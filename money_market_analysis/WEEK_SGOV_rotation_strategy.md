# WEEK/SGOV Dividend Capture Rotation Strategy

**Period:** March 6, 2025 to December 2, 2025
**Initial Investment:** $60,000

## Strategy Overview

This strategy rotates between WEEK (weekly dividends) and SGOV (monthly dividends) to capture both:

- **Base Position:** WEEK for weekly dividend collection
- **Monthly Switch:** Before each SGOV ex-dividend date, switch to SGOV to capture the monthly dividend
- **Return to WEEK:** After capturing monthly dividend, switch back to WEEK before next WEEK-only ex-dividend
- **Overlap Rule:** When both have ex-div same day, stay in SGOV (tiebreaker)
